Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a custom cabinet project?

The timeline for custom cabinet projects varies depending on the scope and complexity. Typically, a standard kitchen cabinet project takes 4-6 weeks from design to installation. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

What materials do you use for your cabinets?

We use high-quality materials including solid hardwoods, plywood, and premium veneers. Our cabinets feature soft-close hinges, full-extension drawer slides, and durable finishes. We can work with various materials based on your preferences and budget.

Do you offer cabinet refacing services?

Yes, we offer cabinet refacing services as a cost-effective alternative to full replacement. This involves replacing doors, drawer fronts, and applying new veneers to existing cabinet boxes while maintaining the current layout.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a comprehensive warranty on all our custom cabinets. Our standard warranty covers manufacturing defects and workmanship for 5 years. For more details, please visit our warranty page.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free in-home consultations and estimates. During the consultation, we'll discuss your needs, take measurements, and provide you with a detailed quote for your project.

Can you match my existing cabinetry style or color?

Absolutely. We can match most existing cabinet styles and finishes. Bring a sample or photo to your consultation, and we'll work to ensure a seamless look.

Do you handle both design and installation?

Yes, we offer a full-service experience, including design, fabrication, and professional installation. Our team will guide you through every step of the process.

Are your cabinets custom-built or pre-fabricated?

All of our cabinets are custom-built to your specifications in our local workshop. This ensures a perfect fit and personalized design for your space.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding communities. Contact us to see if we service your location.

Can you help with cabinet organization solutions?

Yes, we offer a variety of organization options such as pull-out shelves, spice racks, lazy Susans, drawer dividers, and more to maximize your storage space.

Do you offer eco-friendly or sustainable cabinet options?

We offer eco-friendly materials and finishes, including FSC-certified woods and low-VOC finishes. Let us know if you have specific sustainability requirements.

How do I care for and clean my new cabinets?

We recommend cleaning cabinets with a soft cloth and mild soap. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive pads. We'll provide detailed care instructions upon installation.

Can you build cabinets for rooms other than kitchens?

Yes, we design and build cabinets for bathrooms, laundry rooms, offices, mudrooms, garages, and more. Custom storage solutions are available for any space.

Yes, our team can handle the removal and disposal of your old cabinets as part of the installation process.

Can I see samples of your work?

Of course! Please visit our gallery page to view photos of completed projects, or ask to see physical samples during your consultation.

What is the process for getting started?

Contact us to schedule a free consultation. We'll discuss your needs, take measurements, provide design ideas, and give you a detailed quote. Once approved, we'll begin the design and fabrication process.

Are your cabinets made locally?

Yes, all of our cabinets are designed and built locally in our workshop, supporting local jobs and ensuring quality craftsmanship.

Can you work with my contractor or interior designer?

We frequently collaborate with contractors, architects, and interior designers to ensure your project goes smoothly and meets your vision.

What if I have a small or unusually shaped space?

Custom cabinetry is ideal for maximizing storage in small or uniquely shaped spaces. We specialize in creative solutions for challenging layouts.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling us, emailing, or filling out the contact form on our Contact page. We'll get back to you promptly to arrange a convenient time.
