Our Service Areas

Jerry's Cabinets proudly serves the major metropolitan areas of Arizona. Our expert team brings quality craftsmanship and exceptional service to communities throughout the state.

Phoenix, AZ

Our home base and largest service area. We've been serving the Phoenix metropolitan area for over 20 years, providing custom cabinetry solutions to homes and businesses throughout the Valley.

Tucson, AZ

Serving the greater Tucson area with the same commitment to quality and service. Our team regularly travels to Tucson to provide custom cabinet solutions for homes and businesses.

Prescott, AZ

Bringing our expertise to the Prescott area, we provide custom cabinetry solutions that blend perfectly with the region's unique architectural styles and natural surroundings.

Flagstaff, AZ

Serving the Flagstaff community with custom cabinetry solutions that withstand the unique climate and complement the area's distinctive mountain architecture.
