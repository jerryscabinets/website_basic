Our Warranty

At Jerry's Cabinets, we stand behind the quality of our work. Our comprehensive warranty ensures that your investment is protected and that you'll enjoy your cabinets for years to come.

Lifetime Warranty

All our custom cabinets come with a lifetime warranty on materials and workmanship. We guarantee that your cabinets will maintain their structural integrity and finish quality for as long as you own your home.

Installation Guarantee

Our installation work is guaranteed for 5 years. We ensure that your cabinets are installed correctly and will function properly for years to come.

Finish Protection

Our premium finishes are guaranteed against peeling, cracking, or discoloration for 10 years under normal use and maintenance conditions.

Hardware Warranty

All hardware components, including hinges, drawer slides, and handles, are covered by a 5-year warranty against manufacturing defects.

Warranty Terms & Conditions

What's Covered

  • Structural integrity of cabinet boxes and frames
  • Quality of materials and workmanship
  • Proper installation and alignment
  • Finish durability and appearance
  • Hardware functionality

What's Not Covered

  • Damage caused by improper maintenance or cleaning
  • Accidental damage or abuse
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Modifications made after installation
  • Damage from water or moisture not related to manufacturing

Making a Warranty Claim

To make a warranty claim, please contact us with the following information:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Date of installation
  • Description of the issue
  • Photos of the problem area

We will respond to your claim within 48 hours and schedule an inspection if necessary.
