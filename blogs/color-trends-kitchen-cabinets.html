2024 Kitchen Cabinet Color Trends

Choosing the right color for your kitchen cabinets can transform the entire look and feel of your space. In 2024, homeowners are embracing a mix of bold statements and timeless classics. Here are the top color trends to consider for your kitchen renovation:

Earthy Greens

Shades of sage, olive, and forest green are gaining popularity for their calming, organic feel. These colors pair beautifully with natural wood accents and brass hardware, creating a warm and inviting kitchen.

Deep Blues

Navy and midnight blue cabinets offer a sophisticated, dramatic look. These hues work well as an accent island or for all cabinetry, especially when paired with light countertops and gold or matte black hardware.

Warm Neutrals

Beige, taupe, and greige (a blend of gray and beige) are making a comeback. These soft, versatile tones provide a timeless backdrop and complement a variety of design styles, from modern to farmhouse.

Matte Black

For a bold, modern statement, matte black cabinets are on trend. They add depth and contrast, especially in open-concept kitchens, and look stunning with white or wood accents.

Classic Whites

White cabinets remain a favorite for their clean, bright appearance. In 2024, look for creamy, off-white shades that add warmth and softness to the kitchen.

Popular Finishes

  • Matte Finishes: Offer a modern, understated look and are less prone to showing fingerprints.
  • Textured Woods: Visible wood grain and textured finishes add depth and character to cabinets.
  • Two-Tone Designs: Combining two cabinet colors, such as dark lowers and light uppers, creates visual interest and a custom feel.

Design Tips

  • Pair bold cabinet colors with neutral walls and countertops for balance.
  • Use hardware and lighting to complement and enhance your chosen cabinet color.
