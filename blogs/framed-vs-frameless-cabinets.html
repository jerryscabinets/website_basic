Understanding Cabinet Construction

When choosing custom cabinets, one of the fundamental decisions is between framed and frameless construction. Each style has its own aesthetic appeal, accessibility benefits, and structural differences. This post will help you understand these two popular options.

Key Differences and Considerations

Framed cabinets feature a face frame on the front of the cabinet box, to which doors and drawers are attached. Frameless, or European-style, cabinets lack this face frame, offering fuller access to the cabinet interior and a more contemporary look. We'll explore the pros and cons of each to help you decide.
