Kitchens for Everyone: Accessibility in Mind

An accessible kitchen is designed to be usable and comfortable for people of all ages and abilities, including those with mobility challenges. Custom cabinetry plays a crucial role in creating such a space.

Key Features of Accessible Cabinetry

We'll discuss design considerations like varied counter heights, pull-out shelves and drawers for easy access, open knee space under sinks and cooktops, and reachable storage solutions. Thoughtful design can make a kitchen both beautiful and universally functional.

Conclusion

Jerry's Cabinets is experienced in designing custom cabinetry that meets accessibility standards and individual needs, creating kitchens that are welcoming to everyone. Contact us to discuss your accessible design project.
