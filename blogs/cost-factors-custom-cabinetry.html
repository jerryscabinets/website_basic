Understanding Your Investment

Custom cabinetry is a significant investment in your home. Understanding the factors that influence the cost can help you plan your project and make informed decisions that align with your budget and expectations.

What Drives the Cost?

Key cost drivers include the choice of wood and materials, the complexity of the design and construction, the type of finish selected, the hardware options, and the inclusion of special features or accessories. Labor and installation costs also play a significant role.
