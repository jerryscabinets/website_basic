Choosing Your Storage Style

The debate between open shelving and closed cabinets is a common one in kitchen and home design. Both offer distinct advantages and suit different lifestyles and aesthetic preferences. This post explores the pros and cons of each.

Weighing the Options

Open shelving can create an airy, spacious feel and allows for easy access and display of items. However, it requires diligent organization and can accumulate dust. Closed cabinets conceal clutter, offer more protection for contents, but can sometimes make a space feel more enclosed.
