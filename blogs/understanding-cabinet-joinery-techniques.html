The Backbone of Quality Cabinetry

Cabinet joinery refers to the methods used to connect pieces of wood together to form a cabinet. The type and quality of joinery significantly impact the strength, durability, and longevity of your cabinetry.

Common Joinery Techniques

We'll explore traditional and modern joinery techniques, such as dovetail joints (known for their exceptional strength and beauty), mortise and tenon joints, dado joints, and rabbet joints. Understanding these methods helps appreciate the craftsmanship in high-quality cabinets.
