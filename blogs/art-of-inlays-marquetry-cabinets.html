Elevating Cabinetry with Decorative Arts

Inlays and marquetry are sophisticated decorative techniques that involve inserting pieces of contrasting materials (like wood, mother-of-pearl, or metal) into a wood surface to create intricate patterns or pictures. These arts add a level of luxury and personalization to custom cabinetry.

Understanding the Techniques

Inlay typically involves setting pieces into channels or recesses cut into the base wood. Marquetry is a more complex art of creating veneer pictures by fitting together small pieces of colored woods and other materials. We'll explore the history and application of these beautiful crafts in cabinet making.
