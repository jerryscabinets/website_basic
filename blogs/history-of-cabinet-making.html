From Ancient Times to Modern Craft

Cabinet making is an ancient craft with a rich history, evolving alongside human civilization. From early storage chests in ancient Egypt to the ornate furniture of European royalty, the desire for functional and beautiful storage has driven innovation in woodworking.

Evolution of Styles and Techniques

This post will take a brief journey through key periods in cabinet making history, highlighting influential styles like Renaissance, Baroque, Rococo, Shaker, and Art Deco. We'll also touch upon the evolution of tools and techniques that have shaped the craft into what it is today.
