The Importance of Cabinet Finishes

The finish on your cabinets not only enhances their beauty but also protects the wood. From rich stains that highlight wood grain to smooth painted finishes, the options are vast. This guide covers popular finishing techniques.

Exploring Stains, Paints, and Varnishes

We discuss the pros and cons of different finishes: stains for a natural look, paints for a bold color statement, and varnishes for durable protection. Understanding these options helps in making an informed choice for your custom cabinetry.
